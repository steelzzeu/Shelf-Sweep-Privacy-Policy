At Steelzz Development, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension, Shelf Sweep ("the Extension"), handles your information.

1. Information Collection

Shelf Sweep does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. All data processed by the Extension remains locally on your device.

1.1 Local Storage

The Extension stores the following information locally in your browser:

  • User preferences and settings (idle timeout, protected domains, etc.)
  • Information about discarded tabs (URLs, titles, timestamps)
  • Screenshots of discarded tabs (if enabled in settings)
  • Memory usage statistics

1.2 Browser Permissions

Shelf Sweep requires certain permissions to function properly:

  • tabs: To access and manage browser tabs
  • storage: To store settings and discarded tab information locally
  • activeTab: To interact with the current tab
  • tabCapture: To capture screenshots of tabs before discarding them
  • notifications: To display notifications when tabs are discarded

2. Use of Information

The information stored locally is used solely for the following purposes:

  • Providing the core functionality of the Extension (managing and discarding idle tabs)
  • Displaying your discarded tabs and allowing you to restore them
  • Preserving your preferences and settings
  • Showing memory usage statistics

3. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade any information with third parties. Since all data remains on your device, there is no external sharing of your information.

4. Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's secure storage APIs. The Extension follows security best practices to ensure the integrity of your data.

5. Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can clear all stored data at any time through the Extension's options page
  • You can disable or uninstall the Extension at any time
  • You can customize which domains are protected from automatic tab discarding
  • You can enable or disable screenshot capturing

6. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date at the top of this page.
